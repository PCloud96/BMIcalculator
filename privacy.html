Privacy Policy

BMI Calculator Pro · Cloud Motion Lab LLC · Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

BMI Calculator Pro does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter (height, weight, age) is stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers.

2. Health Data

The app calculates BMI based on the height and weight you enter. This data remains on your device at all times. We do not access Apple Health or HealthKit.

3. Advertising

The free version of the app displays ads served by Unity Ads. Unity Ads may collect device identifiers for ad delivery purposes. We do not use App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and do not track users across apps or websites. For Unity's privacy policy, visit unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Analytics

We do not use any analytics services or tracking SDKs. No usage data is collected.

5. Subscriptions & Purchases

Subscription and purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
